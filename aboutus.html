ABOUT US

WE DON'T JUST BUILD PRODUCTS, WE BUILD TRUST.

“Nelco Steel India“, is the fastest growing Manufacturer, and Supplier firm of Stainless Steel, Carbon Steel, Alloy Steel, Copper, Brass, Lead, Aluminium, Monel, Inconel, Hastalloy as per Standards

At Nelco Steel India, The following items we manufacture and deal with are in a wide range of sizes and grades to suit your needs and are readily available with us in bulk quantities.
Pipes & Tubes: Seamless, Welded, Spiral, SAW, Large Diameter Pipes.
Pipe Fittings: Buttweld & Socket weld Elbow (Short & Long Radius), Tee, Reducer, Return Bends, Stub ends, Collar, Union, Cross, Coupling, Bushing, Plug, Swage, Nipples, Bend, Adapter, Weldolet, Elbowlet, Sockolet, Threadolet.
Flanges: Slip-On, Socket weld, Blind, Weld neck, Screwed, Lapped Joint
Plates/Sheets/Coils: Full Plate, Circles, Rings, As per requirement
Bars: Round, Square & Hexagon (Specialist in EN Series & S.S.)
Structure: Angels, Channels & Beams
Wires & Mesh Fasteners:Bolts, Nuts, Washers, Anchors, Eye Bolt, Stud, Threaded Rod Etc.

MATERIALS:
STAINLESS STEEL, CARBON STEEL, ALLOY STEEL, MILD STEEL INCOLOY 800, 825, NICKEL ALLOY 200, 201, TITANIUM GR 2, INCONEL 600, 601, 625,INCOLOY ALLOY 20, HASTELLOY C22, C276, DUPLEX & SUPER DUPLEX, GUN METAL, EN SERIES, PHOSPHORUS BRONZE, BOILER QUALITY, MONEL 400, K500 COPPER, BRASS, 17-4PH & 15-5PH, ALUMINIUM HARDOX(SSAB) 400,500 PLATES, SMO 254 (UNS S31254) ABRASION RESISTANT PLATE (200,400 & 500BHN), SAIL HARD & UTTAM HARD PLATES.

SIZES:
PIPES: OD: 1/8" NB TO 36"NB, SCH 5 TO SCH XXS
TUBES: OD: 6.35MM TO 152MM
PIPE FITTINGS: OD: 1/8" NB TO 36" NB, SCH 5 TO SCH XXS
BARS: DIA: 5MM TO 500MM
FLANGES: 15 NB TO 600 NB 150 CLASS TO 2500 CLASS
