PACKING

Types of Packing :

  1. Bundles (hexagonal)
  2. Wooden Boxes
  3. Crates (steel/wooden)
  4. Special crates for pipes & tubes with each radius baffles
  5. Packing as per customer’s requirements
Special Features :
  1. Each bundle covered with plastic for protection against extreme weather.
  2. End of tubes protected by plastic caps.
  3. Bundles strapped with plastic strips.
  4. Each radius of tube separated by a separator.
  5. Packing list (laminated) placed with box/crate.
